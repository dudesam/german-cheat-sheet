## German Modal Verbs (Modalverben) and Related Verbs

| German | English | Usage |
|--------|---------|-------|
| dürfen | may, to be allowed to | permission |
| können | can, to be able to | ability, possibility |
| sollen | should, to be supposed to | recommendation, duty |
| müssen | must, to have to | necessity, obligation |
| möchten | would like to | polite desire, wish |
| wollen | to want to | desire, intention |
| mögen | to like | preference, polite requests |
| wissen | to know | knowledge, facts |
| werden | will, to become | future tense, change of state |

## Basic Examples

| Modal Verb | German Example | English Translation |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| dürfen | Ihr dürft spielen. | You may play. |
| können | Ich kann schwimmen. | I can swim. |
| sollen | Er soll kommen. | He should come. |
| müssen | Du musst lernen. | You must study. |
| möchten | Ich möchte Tee trinken. | I would like to drink tea. |
| wollen | Wir wollen gehen. | We want to go. |
| mögen | Sie mag Kaffee. | She likes coffee. |
| wissen | Er weiß die Antwort. | He knows the answer. |
| werden | Sie wird Ärztin werden. | She will become a doctor. |
