## German Parts of Day

| English | German | Article | Preposition Usage | Time Range |
|---------|--------|---------|------------------|------------|
| Morning | Morgen | der Morgen | am Morgen | 6:00 - 10:00 |
| Forenoon | Vormittag | der Vormittag | am Vormittag | 10:00 - 12:00 |
| Noon | Mittag | der Mittag | am Mittag | 12:00 - 13:00 |
| Afternoon | Nachmittag | der Nachmittag | am Nachmittag | 13:00 - 18:00 |
| Evening | Abend | der Abend | am Abend | 18:00 - 22:00 |
| Night | Nacht | die Nacht | in der Nacht | 22:00 - 6:00 |
| Midnight | Mitternacht | die Mitternacht | um Mitternacht | 0:00 |

## Alternative Expressions

## Common Time Greetings

| Time Period | German Greeting | Usage |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| Morning | Guten Morgen | Until 10:00 |
| Forenoon/Afternoon | Guten Tag | 10:00 - 18:00 |
| Evening | Guten Abend | From 18:00 |
| Night | Gute Nacht | When going to bed |
